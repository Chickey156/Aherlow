About Aherlow

The Glen of Aherlow, situated in County Tipperary, Ireland

is a stunning, scenic valley nestled between the Galtee Mountains and Slievenamuck ridge

This "walker's paradise" is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts,

offering a range of activities from serene loop walks through the Glen of Aherlow Nature Park

to challenging hikes up the Galtees. The region is rich in history and natural beauty,

featuring landmarks like the Christ the King Statue and the ruins of Moor Abbey.

It provides a peaceful escape with local accommodation options and easy access to historical sites like the Rock of Cashel.

Activites in Aherlow

Cycling & Mountain Biking: Scenic, parallel roads running the length of the glen are ideal for casual cycling,

while forestry tracks offer rugged terrain for mountain bikers.

Looped Walks: The Glen of Aherlow Nature Park offers several family-friendly

low-level looped walks, such as the Woodland, Bianconi, and Ballinacourty loops.
